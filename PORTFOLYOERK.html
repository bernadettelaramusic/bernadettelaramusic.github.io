PORTFOLYO İÇERİK

ABOUT ME

I am a senior bachelor’s degree student in Music Technologies - Sound Design at Istanbul Technical University in the faculty of Turkish Music State Conservatory. I also work as a Junior Sound Engineer at JIB Games. I make alternative pop music as “Bernadette Lara” and additionally work on my other projects on my YouTube and SoundCloud channel as “bernadette”. “bernadetta” is a project where I focus on sound design, foley and acousmatic music.

I was born in Istanbul, Turkey on the 23rd of October, 2000. I was classically trained on piano as a child and then began composing my own songs at the age of 13. At high school I performed with numerous bands, which led me to recording my own songs. When my bandmates wanted me to record a demo version of my own songs, I met sound engineering and I knew I wanted to work with sound my whole life. I started preparing for the special aptitude test for Istanbul Technical University’s Music Technologies - Sound Design undergraduate program.

My journey began when I got accepted to the program. I had the chance to meet and work with esteemed musicians, sound engineers and colleagues during my time in university. My professors made me realize there was more to sound than music. At first, I was merely willing to produce my own music. My network at the university and the classes I took made me see the world of sound in a more open view. I took classes such as Studio Acoustics, Sound Design, Electroacoustic Composition, Computer Music and many more.
